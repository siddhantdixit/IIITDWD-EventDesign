Call for Paper

The conference invites submissions on all research related to the intersection of computer science, game theory, economics, operations research, social sciences, and society and technology, both theory and development, and deployment. The conference looks for rigorous research contributions as well as highly creative ideas.

Topics of Interest

The conference seeks interdisciplinary contributions from a wide range of domains including computer science, economics, human factors, sociology are encouraged. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following areas.
